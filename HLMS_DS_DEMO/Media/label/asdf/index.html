Soft Transparency (Alpha)



Transparency is realised by dithering, which means just rejecting every second pixel for 0.5% opacity, every 3rd for 0.333%, etc..

its not the optimal solution and can lead to ugly results, but is still somewhat usefull for large objects, fading in distant obects coming closer, teleport effects and perhaps holorgrams.

But generally its better to use forwarded objects for alpha blended transparency.
